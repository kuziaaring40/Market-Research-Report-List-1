<p><p>The fluidized bed dryer market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Vibrated fluid beds enhance uniformity by inducing vibration. Fluid-bed granulation combines drying and granulation processes for efficient particle formation. Spouted bed dryers utilize a jet of air to fluidize materials, offering high drying rates. Mechanically agitated fluid-bed dryers incorporate agitation for improved mixing. Centrifugal fluid-bed dryers employ centrifugal force for enhanced particle separation and drying. Fluidized spray dryers integrate atomization to create fine powders, suitable for various industries.</p></p>

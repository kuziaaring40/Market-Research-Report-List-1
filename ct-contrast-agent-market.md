<p><strong>The CT Contrast Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Iodine Based</li><li>Barium Sulfate Compounds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT contrast agent market is primarily divided into iodine-based agents and barium sulfate compounds. Iodine-based contrast agents are widely used for enhancing the visibility of vascular structures and organs in computed tomography scans, with applications in both intravascular and extravascular imaging. Barium sulfate compounds, on the other hand, are specifically utilized for gastrointestinal imaging, helping to delineate the digestive tract. Both types play crucial roles in diagnostic imaging, enhancing clarity and accuracy during medical examinations.</p></p>

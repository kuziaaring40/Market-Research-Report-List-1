<p><p>The Oil and Gas Pipeline Leak Detection Market comprises various technologies designed to identify leaks efficiently. Mass-Volume Balance methods calculate discrepancies between input and output volumes to detect leaks. Acoustic/Ultrasonic systems utilize sound waves to detect abnormal noises associated with leaks. Fiber Optics leverage light transmission through optical fibers to identify flow disruptions. Vapor Sensing involves monitoring gaseous emissions to identify potential leaks. Each method contributes to enhancing safety and minimizing environmental impact in pipeline operations.</p></p>

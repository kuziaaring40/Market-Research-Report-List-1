<p><ul><li>CCD Chip</li><li>CMOS Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital photosensitive material market primarily consists of two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) chips. CCD chips are known for their high image quality and low noise, making them ideal for professional photography and scientific applications. Conversely, CMOS chips offer faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and integration capabilities, making them widely used in consumer electronics like smartphones and webcams. Both technologies play critical roles in capturing images, each catering to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>

<p><p>UTP (Unshielded Twisted Pair) cables are essential for networking, ranging from CAT5 to CAT7. CAT5 cables support speeds up to 100 Mbps over distances of 100 meters. CAT5e enhances performance to 1 Gbps, reducing crosstalk. CAT6 offers improved shielding and can handle 10 Gbps at shorter distances. CAT6a extends this capability up to 10 Gbps over 100 meters. CAT7 further enhances performance with better shielding, supporting up to 10 Gbps over longer distances, making it suitable for high-speed networks.</p></p>

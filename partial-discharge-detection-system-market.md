<p><p>The Partial Discharge Detection System market is divided into two main types: online and offline detection. Online detection involves continuous monitoring of electrical equipment during operation, enabling real-time identification of partial discharges, which can indicate insulation deterioration. Offline detection, on the other hand, occurs when the equipment is de-energized, using specialized testing methods to assess insulation integrity and detect discharges. Both methods are essential for ensuring the reliability and longevity of electrical systems, minimizing downtime and maintenance costs.</p></p>

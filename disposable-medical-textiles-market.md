<p><p>Disposable medical textiles are essential in healthcare, primarily categorized into non-woven and woven fabrics. Non-woven fabrics, made from fibers bonded together through methods like thermal or chemical processes, offer high absorbency and barrier properties, making them ideal for surgical gowns and masks. Woven fabrics, created from interlacing threads, provide strength and durability, suitable for applications such as drapes and sheets. Both types play crucial roles in infection control and patient safety in medical settings.</p></p>

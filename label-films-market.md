<p><ul><li>Conventional Label Film</li><li>IML Label Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Label films are categorized into two main types: Conventional Label Films and In-Mold Label (IML) Films. Conventional Label Films are typically made from materials like polypropylene or polyethylene and are directly adhered to products, used widely for branding and information. In contrast, IML Label Films are integrated into the product during the molding process, offering superior durability and aesthetic appeal. Both types serve various industries, enhancing product visibility and providing essential information while adhering to packaging standards.</p></p>

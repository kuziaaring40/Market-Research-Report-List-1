<p><p>The oil spill cleanup and remediation market can be categorized into three main types: physical, chemical, and biological. </p><p>Physical methods involve the use of barriers and skimmers to contain and remove oil from water surfaces. Chemical methods utilize dispersants and surfactants to break down oil into smaller droplets, facilitating natural degradation. Biological remediation employs microorganisms to naturally metabolize oil, promoting environmental restoration. Mechanical solutions incorporate equipment for efficient removal and containment, ensuring effective response to oil spills across various environments.</p></p>

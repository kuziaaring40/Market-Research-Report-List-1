<p><p>The Leuco Dye market is segmented into two primary types: touch-activated and cold-activated. Touch-activated dyes change color when pressure is applied, making them ideal for applications in products like thermochromic inks and temperature-sensitive labels. Cold-activated dyes, on the other hand, react to temperature changes, revealing color when exposed to cold conditions. This characteristic is particularly useful in items such as cold drink labels and temperature-sensitive packaging, enhancing user interaction and functionality in various consumer goods.</p></p>

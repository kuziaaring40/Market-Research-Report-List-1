<p><ul><li>RKN Type</li><li>RAP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Passive Temperature-Controlled Containers market is primarily divided into two types: RKN (Reefer Known) and RAP (Reefer Active). RKN containers utilize insulation and phase change materials to maintain temperature stability without external power, making them ideal for transporting pharmaceuticals and sensitive goods. In contrast, RAP containers incorporate active cooling systems to actively regulate temperature, providing more precise control but often requiring power sources. Both types cater to the growing demand for reliable temperature-sensitive logistics in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The multiphoton microscopy market is categorized into three main types: two-photon excitation, three-photon excitation, and others. Two-photon excitation employs infrared light to excite fluorophores, enabling deeper tissue imaging with reduced phototoxicity. Three-photon excitation utilizes longer wavelengths for even deeper penetration and reduced scattering, enhancing visualization of complex structures. The "others" category includes various advanced techniques and hybrid systems that combine multiple imaging modalities for specialized applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and biotechnology.</p></p>

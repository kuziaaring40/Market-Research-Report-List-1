<p><ul><li>Hammer Drills</li><li>Impact Drills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corded drills are essential power tools in construction and DIY projects, typically categorized into hammer drills and impact drills. Hammer drills combine rotational and hammering action, making them ideal for drilling into hard materials like concrete and masonry. Impact drills, on the other hand, deliver rotational force in bursts, effectively driving screws and fastening materials quickly without straining the user. Both types offer durability and consistent power, catering to different drilling and fastening needs in various applications.</p></p>

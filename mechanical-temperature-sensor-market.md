<p><strong>The Mechanical Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-Type</li><li>Noncontact-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical temperature sensor market is divided into two main types: contact-type and noncontact-type sensors. Contact-type sensors, like thermocouples and RTDs, measure temperature by direct contact with the object, providing precise readings in various applications. In contrast, noncontact-type sensors, such as infrared thermometers, detect temperature from a distance, ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare, each suited to specific temperature monitoring needs and conditions.</p></p>

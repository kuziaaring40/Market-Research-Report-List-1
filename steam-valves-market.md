<p><p>The steam valves market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic steam valves, electric steam valves, and self-operated steam valves. Pneumatic steam valves utilize compressed air for operation, ensuring rapid response and efficiency in high-pressure applications. Electric steam valves rely on electric actuators, offering precise control and remote operation capabilities. Self-operated steam valves function without external power sources, utilizing the steam pressure itself for actuation, making them ideal for automatic operations in various industrial settings. Each type caters to specific operational needs and applications.</p></p>

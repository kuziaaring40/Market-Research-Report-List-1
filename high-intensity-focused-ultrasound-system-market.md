<p><p>The High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) System market can be categorized into two primary types: Ultrasound-Guided and MR-Guided. Ultrasound-Guided HIFU utilizes real-time ultrasound imaging to precisely target tissues for focused treatment, often employed in urological applications. Conversely, MR-Guided HIFU integrates magnetic resonance imaging to enhance targeting accuracy, providing detailed soft tissue visualization. This method is widely used for treating tumors, as it helps in monitoring and adapting treatment during the procedure, improving overall patient outcomes.</p></p>

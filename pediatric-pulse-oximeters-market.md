<p><p>The pediatric pulse oximeters market includes two primary sensor types: wrap sensors and clip sensors. Wrap sensors are designed to encircle a child's finger or toe, ensuring a secure fit and accurate readings, particularly in younger patients. Clip sensors, on the other hand, attach directly to a fingertip and offer quick application and portability. Both types prioritize comfort and reliability, catering to the unique needs of pediatric patients while facilitating continuous monitoring of oxygen saturation levels and heart rates.</p></p>

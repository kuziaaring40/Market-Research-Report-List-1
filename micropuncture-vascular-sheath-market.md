<p><p>The Micropuncture Vascular Sheath market is segmented into three main types: straight-through, rotating, and locking sheaths. Straight-through sheaths facilitate direct access, offering minimal resistance for catheter insertion. Rotating sheaths enable multidirectional maneuverability, enhancing navigation during procedures. Locking sheaths feature mechanisms that secure components in place, providing stability during interventions. Each type is designed to improve the efficiency and safety of vascular access, catering to different procedural needs and enhancing overall patient outcomes.</p></p>

ELISA kits employ enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays to quantify cytotoxic effects. Crystal violet kits stain viable cells, and minimal inhibitory concentration kits determine the lowest concentration of a substance that inhibits cell growth, catering to diverse research needs.</p></p>

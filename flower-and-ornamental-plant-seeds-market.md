<p><p>The Flower and Ornamental Plant Seeds Market can be categorized into three main types: annual, biennial, and perennial seeds. Annual seeds produce plants that complete their life cycle in one growing season, often blooming beautifully the same year they are sown. Biennial seeds require two growing seasons to mature, typically blooming in the second year. Perennial seeds grow into plants that live for several years, regrowing each season and providing long-term beauty in gardens and landscapes.</p></p>

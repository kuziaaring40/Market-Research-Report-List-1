<p><p>The High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) film market is classified into two primary types based on light transmission: translucent and opaque. Translucent HDPE films allow some light to pass through, making them suitable for applications requiring visibility alongside protection, such as greenhouse covers. Opaque HDPE films, on the other hand, block light completely, providing privacy and shielding from UV rays, making them ideal for packaging, construction, and waste management purposes. Both types cater to diverse industries with specific needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The heat shrink tube market comprises various materials, each serving specific applications. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) is flexible and has good transparency, often used in crafts and packaging. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective and widely used in electrical insulation and general protection. Each type caters to diverse industry needs.</p></p>

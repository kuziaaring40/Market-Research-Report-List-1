<p><strong>The Mask Exposure Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E-Beam Mask Exposure</li><li>Laser Beam Mask Exposure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Exposure Equipment market comprises two primary types: E-Beam Mask Exposure and Laser Beam Mask Exposure. E-Beam Mask Exposure utilizes electron beams to write patterns onto a resist-coated substrate, offering high precision and resolution, making it ideal for complex semiconductor fabrication. Conversely, Laser Beam Mask Exposure employs focused lasers to expose the resist, allowing for faster processing speeds and flexibility in design changes. Both techniques are crucial in producing advanced photomasks for integrated circuits and nanotechnology applications.</p></p>
